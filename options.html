Keyboard Shortcuts

Windows or Linux

Ctrl + Shift + 1 Jump to open note 1
Ctrl + Shift + 2 Jump to open note 2
Ctrl + Shift + 3 Jump to open note 3

Mac

Option + 1 Jump to open note 1
Option + 2 Jump to open note 2
Option + 3 Jump to open note 3
Option + F Toggle Focus mode

Options

Hides bottom panel (Options link and page number) to give you more editing space.
To change this option back, visit Options (right-click on My Notes icon in browser's toolbar), or press "Toggle Focus mode" keyboard shortcut.
Creates and maintains a backup of My Notes in your Google Drive under the folder My Notes.
A permission will be requested during the process.
If there is a backup left by a previous installation, it will be used. If you had some local notes already, they will be added to the backup.
